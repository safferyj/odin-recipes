Salmon Patties

Description

An easy and nutritious base to build a meal around. Pair with spinach and avocado or your favourite salad.

Ingredients

  • 3 x 415 g tins of Red Salmon
  • 2 kg of Potatoes for mashing
  • Butter, Milk for mashed potatoes
  • 3 x large Eggs
  • 2 x 320 g packets of Beetroot, Carrot and Cabbage coleslaw mix
  • 2 cups of Peas
  • ¼ cup of fried Shallots or Onions
  • 2 tablespoons of Olive oil
  • Plain Flour for dusting
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for dusting

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200 °C
  2. Make mashed Potatoes
  3. Drain liquid from Salmon
  4. Microwave Beetroot, Carrot and Cabbage and Peas for 1 minute
  5. Thoroughly mix all ingredients (except Flour and Parmesan) together in a large bowl
  6. Line a tray with baking paper
  7. Form patties on the tray
  8. Dust patties with Flour and Parmesan
  9. Bake in oven for 30 minutes
