Sourdough Breakfast

Desciption

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes, coloured baby, one each of red, orange and yellow
  • Cumcumber
  • Pistacios (unsalted) x 4
  • Olives, dry Kalamata x 4
  • Topping, one of
    • Hommus
    • Avocado
    • Cottage cheese
  • Seasoning
    • Basil, fresh leaves
    • Paprika, smoked
  • Sourdough bread, white

Steps

  1. Slice Sourdough bread and place in toaster
  2. Cut off about an inch of Cumcumber, wash, slice into 4 wedges and place on plate
  3. Wash Tomatoes and place on plate
  4. Place Olives and Pistacios on plate
  5. Check toaster, remove Sourdough toast once done, place on plate
  6. Apply chosen Topping to Sourdough toast
  7. Garnish with Basil and Paprika
